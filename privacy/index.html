Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Bike App ("the App") collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive your name and email address. This is used to create and identify your account.

Routes and Places

Routes you plan and spots you add are stored on our servers so you can access them across devices.

Moments

Text notes, photos, and voice recordings you create are stored on our servers. Voice recordings may be sent to OpenAI's Whisper service for transcription. Photos are stored securely and only accessible to you.

Location Data

If you grant location permission, the App accesses your device's GPS to show your position on the map and attach location coordinates to moments and spots. Location data is only collected while you actively use the App (not in the background).

Unit Preferences

Your preferred measurement units (metric/imperial, temperature) are stored on our servers to sync across devices.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the App's functionality
  • To sync your data across devices
  • To transcribe voice notes (via OpenAI Whisper)
  • To improve the App through anonymized analytics

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • Google Maps & Places API — map tiles, place search. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics — anonymized usage analytics and crash reports. Subject to Firebase Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat — subscription management. Subject to RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.
  • OpenAI Whisper — voice transcription. Audio is sent to OpenAI for processing. Subject to OpenAI's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers. We use HTTPS for all communications between the App and our servers. Authentication tokens are stored securely in your device's keychain/keystore.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained as long as your account exists. You can delete your account at any time from Settings, which will permanently delete all your data including routes, spots, moments, and account information. This action cannot be undone.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data (visible in the App)
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Revoke location permissions at any time via device settings

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App or by other means.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at guillem@bikeapp.dev.
